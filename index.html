Our vision

DeafBoard aims to facilitate the professional integration of the deaf and dumb through a platform offering translation into sign language and vice versa.

Website

Our website offers a better communication on both sides thanks to our intelligent motion recognition system. The user can also pre-record responses in order to facilitate the discussion. It is possible to talk and the application will then be able to write any word.

Features

Speech To Sign

Our Speech To Sign solution allows the translation of spoken words into sign language by a mime who will be able to reproduce them in sign language

Sign To Speech

Our Sign To Speech solution uses video recognition to translate sign language so that it is audible.

Speech To Text

Our Speech to Text solution will write down each of the words spoken by the user in order to be understood by everyone.
